Stopping Fermentation
Potassium Sorbate: *1/2 tsp per UK Gallon
Campden Tablet: 1 crushed tablet per UK Gallon
or Potassium(/sodium) Metabisulphite: 1/4 tsp (1.5g) per 5 UK Gallons


Sterilising Solution
Potassium(/sodium) Metabisulphite: 2 tablespoons per UK Gallon (this solution may keep for a while if properly sealed)
If using a dedicated brand cleaner/steriliser refer to the instructions on the pack.


Additives
Citric Acid: **1 tsp for a must containing some acid, 3 tsp for must containing none. 1 tsp = roughly 1 lemon
Tannin: **1/2 tsp or 1 mug of strong black tea (bag steeped for 15 mins) per UK Gallon
Glycerine: **1 tsp per UK Gallon
Bentonite: 1/2 tsp per UK Gallon
Nutrient: Refer to instructions on the pack.
Pectolase: ***1 tsp per UK Gallon


Flavourings
Dried Elderflowers: ****10-15g per UK Gallon
Oak Chippings(not dust): ****x per UK Gallon for x months during maturation


Notes:
*When adding Potassium Sorbate you always need to add the appropriate amount of Campden Tablets or Metabisulphite.
**Actual amount needed will depend on how much is naturally present in the must and the desired end result.
***Only used where the fruit contains large amounts of Pectin.
****Actual amount needed will depend on individual taste and the desired end result.
