Type Predominant Acids* Present
Apple Malic, quinic, aketoglutaric, oxalacetic, citric, pyruvic, fumaric, lactic and succinic acids.
Apricot Malic and Citric acids.
Avocado Tartaric acid.
Banana Malic, citric, tartaric and traces of acetic and formic acids.
Beans Citric, malic and small amounts of succinic and fumaric acids.
Bilberry Citric acid.
Blackberry Isocitric, Malic, lactoisocitric, shikimic, quinic, and traces of citric and oxalic acids.
Blueberry Citric, malic, glyceric, citramalic, glycolic, succinic, glucuronic, galacturonic, shikimic, quinic, glutamic and aspartic acids.
Boysenberry Citric, malic, and isocitric acids.
Broccoli Malic and Citric (3:2), oxalic and succinic acids.
Carrot Malic, Citric, Isocitric, succinic and fumaric acids.
Cherry Malic, citric, tartaric, succinic, quinic, shikimic, glyceric and glycolic acids.
Crabapple Malic acid.
Cranberry Citric, malic and benzoic acids.
Currants Citric, Tartaric, malic and succinic acids.
Elderberry Citric, malic, shikimic and quinic acids.
Fig Citric, malic and acetic acids.
Gooseberry Citric, malic, shikimic and quinic acids.
Grape Malic and Tartaric (3:2), citric and oxalic acids.
Grapefruit Citric, tartaric, malic and oxalic acids.
Kiwi Citric acid.
Kumquat Citric acid.
Lemon Citric, malic, tartaric and oxalic acids (no Isocitric acid)
Lime Citric, malic, tartaric and oxalic acids.
Loganberry Malic, citric acids.
Mushroom Lactarimic, cetostearic, fumaric and allantoic acids.
Nectarine Malic acid.
Orange Citric, malic and oxalic acids.
Orange Peel Malic, citric and oxalic acids.
Passion Fruit Malic acid.
Peach Malic and citric acids.
Pear Malic, citric, tartaric and oxalic acids.
Peas Malic acid.
Pineapple Citric and Malic acids.
Plum Malic, tartaric and oxalic acids.
Pomegranate Citric and Malic acids.
Potato Malic, Citric, oxalic, phosphoric and pyroglutamic acids.
Raspberry Citric acid.
Rhubarb Malic, Citric and Oxalic acids.
Rosehip Malic acid.
Quince Malic acid (no citric acid).
Salal Citric and malic acids.
Strawberry Citric, malic, shikimic, succinic, glyceric, glycolic and aspartic acids.
Tangerine Citric acid.
Tomato Citric, Malic, oxalic, succinic, glycolic, tartaric, phosphoric, hydrochloric, sulphuric, fumaric, pyrrolidinonecarboxylic and galacturonic acids.
Youngberry Citric, malic and isocitric acids.

* Acids which occur in appreciable quantities are shown in italics. The relative amount of each varies widely with the variety, degree of ripeness and seasonal influences. Complete identifications of all the acids present in many of the products is obviously lacking in many instances.
